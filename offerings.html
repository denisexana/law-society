What We Offer

Comprehensive Support for Your Legal Journey

What We Offer

Our society organises regular workshops, guest lectures by legal professionals, networking events, and social activities. We also offer opportunities for students to take part in national and international legal competitions, helping them gain practical experience and professional connections.

Professional Development

Legal Writing Workshops

Enhance your legal writing skills with expert-led workshops covering memorandum writing, case briefs, and professional correspondence.

Guest Lectures

Regular talks by practicing lawyers, judges, and legal professionals sharing insights from their careers.

CV & Interview Preparation

Workshops to help you create compelling CVs and prepare for legal job interviews.

Career Counseling

One-on-one guidance to help you navigate your legal career path and make informed decisions.

Networking & Connections

Professional Networking Events

Monthly meetups with legal professionals from various sectors and practice areas.

Alumni Network

Connect with Surrey Law graduates who are now practicing lawyers across the UK and internationally.

Industry Visits

Organized visits to law firms, courts, and legal organizations to see the profession in action.

Mentorship Program

Pairing students with experienced legal professionals for guidance and career advice.

Competitions & Competitions

Moot Court Competitions

Annual competitions to develop advocacy skills, with prizes and recognition for top performers.

National Competitions

Represent Surrey at national and international legal competitions and mooting events.

Debating Society

Regular debating sessions on current legal issues and policy matters.

Legal Research Projects

Collaborative research projects on contemporary legal issues with publication opportunities.

Social & Community

Social Events

Regular social gatherings, parties, and activities to build friendships and community spirit.

Study Groups

Organized study sessions and peer support groups for different modules and exam preparation.

Charity Work

Volunteer opportunities and fundraising events to give back to the community.

Cultural Events

Celebrations of diversity and cultural awareness within the law school community.
