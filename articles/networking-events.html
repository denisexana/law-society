About Our Networking Series

Our monthly networking events bring together law students, practicing lawyers, judges, and legal professionals from various sectors. These events provide invaluable opportunities to build relationships, gain insights into different legal careers, and discover internship and job opportunities.

Upcoming Events

September: 'Meet the Barristers' - Guildford Crown Court
October: 'In-House Counsel Panel' - London Legal District
November: 'International Law Perspectives' - University Campus
December: 'Holiday Networking Mixer' - Guildford Town Centre

Event Format

• 6:00-6:30 PM: Registration and welcome drinks
• 6:30-7:30 PM: Panel discussion or keynote speaker
• 7:30-8:30 PM: Networking session with professionals
• 8:30-9:00 PM: Informal conversations and follow-ups

What to Expect

• Professional dress code required
• Business cards encouraged
• Pre-event preparation materials provided
• Post-event follow-up guidance
• LinkedIn group access for continued networking

Success Stories

Previous attendees have secured:
• Summer internships at top law firms
• Mentorship opportunities with senior lawyers
• Job offers upon graduation
• Valuable career advice and guidance
