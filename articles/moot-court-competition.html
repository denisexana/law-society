Annual Moot Court Competition

March 15-20, 2025 • University of Surrey, Moot Court Room • 6 days

March 15-20, 2025
University of Surrey, Moot Court Room
6 days
Senior Barristers and Judges

Competition Overview

Our annual moot court competition is the highlight of the academic year, providing students with the opportunity to argue real legal cases before experienced judges and barristers. This prestigious event tests your advocacy skills, legal knowledge, and courtroom presence.

Competition Structure

• Preliminary Rounds: March 15-17 (all participants)
• Quarter Finals: March 18 (top 8 teams)
• Semi Finals: March 19 (top 4 teams)
• Grand Final: March 20 (championship match)

Case Materials

• Real appellate court cases
• Comprehensive case bundles provided
• 4 weeks preparation time
• Access to law library resources
• Mentoring from previous winners

Prizes and Recognition

• 1st Place: £500 cash prize + trophy
• 2nd Place: £300 cash prize + certificate
• 3rd Place: £200 cash prize + certificate
• Best Speaker Award: £100 + special recognition
• All finalists receive certificates for CVs

Judging Criteria

• Legal argument quality (40%)
• Advocacy skills and presentation (30%)
• Response to judicial questions (20%)
• Professional conduct (10%)

Registration

Teams of 2-3 students. Registration opens January 15, 2025. Early registration recommended as spaces are limited.
