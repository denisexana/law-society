Legal Writing Workshop Series

September 15-30, 2024 • University of Surrey, Law Building • 2 weeks

September 15-30, 2024
University of Surrey, Law Building
2 weeks
Prof. Sarah Mitchell, QC

Overview

Our comprehensive legal writing workshop series is designed to transform your legal writing skills from basic to professional level. Whether you're a first-year student or preparing for your final year, this workshop will provide you with the essential tools and techniques needed for effective legal communication.

What You'll Learn

• Legal memorandum writing and structure
• Case brief analysis and presentation
• Contract drafting fundamentals
• Legal research methodology
• Citation and referencing standards
• Professional legal correspondence

Workshop Schedule

Week 1: Foundations of Legal Writing
• Day 1-2: Legal writing principles and structure
• Day 3-4: Research methods and case analysis
• Day 5: Practice session and feedback

Week 2: Advanced Applications
• Day 1-2: Contract drafting and negotiation
• Day 3-4: Legal memorandum writing
• Day 5: Final project and certification

Benefits

• Certificate of completion for your CV
• Sample writing portfolio for job applications
• Networking with legal professionals
• Practical skills for moot court competitions
• Enhanced academic performance

Registration

Limited to 25 students per session. Early registration recommended. Contact us for group discounts and payment plans.
