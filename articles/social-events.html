Social Committee

Welcome Week Events

Kick off the academic year with our exciting welcome week activities:
• Freshers' Party at the Student Union
• Campus tour and scavenger hunt
• Meet and greet with society members
• Pizza and movie night

Regular Social Activities

• Weekly coffee mornings in the law building
• Monthly pub quizzes at local venues
• Seasonal celebrations (Halloween, Christmas, Easter)
• Sports events and team building activities
• Cultural celebrations and international food nights

Special Events

• End of semester celebration dinners
• Summer garden party
• Charity fundraising events
• Alumni networking socials
• Inter-university law society competitions

Benefits of Participation

• Build lasting friendships with fellow students
• Reduce stress and improve mental wellbeing
• Develop teamwork and leadership skills
• Create memories that last beyond university
• Network in a relaxed, social environment

Getting Involved

• Join our social media groups for updates
• Volunteer to help organize events
• Suggest new activities and ideas
• Attend our social committee meetings
• Follow us on Instagram for event photos
